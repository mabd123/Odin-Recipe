Fryed Egg

This recipe describes how to make a standard government issued fryed egg according to BS EN 123554:441

Ingredients

  • Tablespoon of olive oil
  • Egg (preferably chicken or ostrich)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to pan
  2. Crack egg into pan>
  3. Add salt and pepper to egg
  4. Enjoy!
