Spiced Tea

This recipe shows yoi how to make a traditional somali black tea infused with spices

Ingredients

  • Black tea bags
  • Boiling Water
  • Spice mix (Cinnamon poweder, Cardamom, Ginger,
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. prepare cup by adding sugar, spices and tea bag
  3. add hot water to cup
  4. add a splash of milk
  5. Enjoy!
