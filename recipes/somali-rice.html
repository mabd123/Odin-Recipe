Somali Rice

This popular spiced Somali rice dish is known as bariis iyo hilib. In construction, it is very similar to East African pilau rice, however, instead of using vegetable broth or water to cook the rice, this dish generally uses meat broth to add more flavor.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Secret spice mix
  • Lamb meat broth

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and fry in deep pan unit browned
  2. Add secret spice mix and washed rice
  3. Add lamb meat broth and cook unit rice is done
  4. Enjoy
